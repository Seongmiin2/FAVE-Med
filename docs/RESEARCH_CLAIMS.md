# Research claims

## Pre-registered questions

1. Does typed applicability verification improve evidence selection beyond relevance or factuality filtering?
2. Does verification-gated deterministic execution improve answer robustness and reduce unsafe answers?
3. Does the effect reproduce across Telecom and Medical quantitative QA?
4. Is the improvement concentrated on true-but-inapplicable evidence?
5. How well does the method transfer to unseen formula or calculator families?

## Allowed current claim

The repository is an advanced engineering prototype with a successful three-item real-model pilot. No benchmark, cross-domain performance, human-agreement, clinical-validity, or statistical-superiority claim is currently supported.

## Target method

Typed FAVE-DeMo combines semantic parsing, typed requirement/evidence signatures, deterministic compatibility constraints, an execution gate, deterministic execution, and post-execution validation.
