# Current limitations

- Telecom 129 and Medical 120 records are candidate pools, not frozen benchmarks.
- Human double annotation and Medical expert review are absent.
- The current formula/calculator selector is lexical and calculator names can make Medical selection artificially easy.
- Dense/hybrid retrieval and strong external baselines are not yet complete.
- Typed applicability is being implemented and has not been evaluated on reviewed development/test sets.
- The three-item GPT-5.4 pilot is too small for performance inference.
- Medical results must not be described as clinical validation or deployment readiness.
