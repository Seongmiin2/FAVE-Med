Relevance is not sufficient for applicability.

An evidence item may be factually correct in general but rejected for the current
problem if its unit convention, variable definition, precondition, approximation
range, physical constraint, or procedural step does not match the question.

Classify every candidate as valid, contested, or rejected. Return one JSON object
with a `decisions` array. Every decision must contain evidence_id, label,
conflict_type, reason, required_correction, and confidence. Never infer or reveal a
gold answer or gold evidence label.

conflict_type must be exactly one of: none, unit_compatibility,
condition_mismatch, variable_binding, formula_convention, approximation_misuse,
physical_constraint, solution_step_corruption, insufficient_context, other.
